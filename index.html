About Us

Learn why we are the leading technical repair training institute

Building Technical Excellence Since 2010

TechRepair Institute was founded with a vision to create skilled technicians who can meet the growing demand for electronic repair services. With over a decade of experience, we've trained more than 5,000 students who now run successful businesses or work with leading service centers.

Our courses combine theoretical knowledge with extensive hands-on training. We believe in learning by doing, which is why 70% of our course duration is dedicated to practical sessions with real devices.

Our instructors are industry veterans with an average of 15+ years of experience in their respective fields. They bring real-world insights and the latest industry techniques to the classroom.
